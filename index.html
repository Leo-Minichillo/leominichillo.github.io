Beat the Spread

Spreads calculated from 2025-26 nfelo ratings + EPA per play

Each matchup simulates an NFL game using nfelo power ratings blended with EPA per play (offense & defense) to calculate a true fair-value spread. Home field is worth ~1.7 points.

The posted spread is based on that fair value, but it's intentionally biased up to ±4 points. Your job is to spot when the line is off. When you see a big misprice, bet heavy — when it looks fair, bet small or skip.

After each bet, you'll see the fair value spread — compare it to the posted line to see whether you had the edge. The green P&L line tracks your actual results; the yellow dashed line tracks expected P&L based on each bet's implied edge.

Scores use a bivariate normal model around EPA-derived team means (~10.5 pt std dev). Green = covered, Red = didn’t. Think like a sharp.
